# AI Agency Pitch One-Pager

## Brief
Sales one-pager for AI agency services targeting local service businesses.

## Target Audience
- Plumbers, dentists, lawyers, contractors
- Small business owners who miss calls after hours
- Non-technical decision makers

## Goal
Book free discovery calls via a compelling, problem-focused pitch document.

## Brand Feel
- Premium, trustworthy, modern
- Dark theme with amber CTA
- Outcome-focused (not tool-focused)
